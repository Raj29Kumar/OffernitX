Frequently asked questions

Short, direct answers — so people can decide fast.

Does OffernitX send emails automatically?

No. It always creates a Gmail draft. You review and send.

Can OffernitX invent a price or product?

No. It uses only ERP/MRP (single source of truth).

How fast can we deploy it?

Usually in days — depending on how clean your price data is.

What if the inquiry is unclear?

It flags unclear items for review or asks for clarification — it doesn’t guess.

Is it built for Slovakia & Czechia?

Yes — language, workflows and support are designed locally.

Can it run on-prem / locally?

Yes — deployment can be self-hosted, on-prem, or a local Linux app.
